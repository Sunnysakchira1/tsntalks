About the Show

Spotlighting the voices
that shape Thailand

TSN Talks is Thailand's premier Indian voices interview series — a bold, exclusive platform spotlighting the entrepreneurs, community leaders, diplomats, and changemakers driving the Thai-Indian story forward.

Streamed live and on-demand across Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, every episode reaches a highly engaged, culturally connected audience that no other platform in Thailand can access.

Presented by Thai Sikh News — the largest Indian-Thai Facebook community in Thailand with over 50,000 followers and 1.2 million monthly views.
