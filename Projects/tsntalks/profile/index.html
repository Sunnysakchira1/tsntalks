About the Show

Unheard stories.
Authentic voices.

TSN Talks is Thailand's premier interview series spotlighting the entrepreneurs, community leaders, diplomats, and changemakers who shape the Thai-Indian world — one authentic conversation at a time.

Streaming across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook, every episode uncovers the real stories behind success — the failures, the pivots, and the moments that define a life in business.

Presented by Thai Sikh News — the largest Indian-Thai media platform in Thailand. Built on community trust, not advertising budgets.
